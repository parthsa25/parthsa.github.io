About Me

Hi, I am Parth.

I am a highly motivated Mechanical Engineer with over four years of experience in new product development, specializing in mechanical design, product testing, and advanced manufacturing. My expertise includes CAD, FEA, CFD, and additive manufacturing, with a strong emphasis on design for manufacturability (DFM) and design for assembly (DFA). Throughout my career, I have successfully led the design and development of electro-mechanical systems, including high-voltage battery packs, LED lighting, vacuum systems, and audio devices. I have a proven track record of driving cost efficiencies, enhancing product performance, and delivering innovative engineering solutions.

My ability to collaborate with cross-functional teams in fast-paced environments enables me to bring consumer-focused products from concept to production seamlessly. Beyond my professional endeavors, I am deeply passionate about prototyping and additive manufacturing. I actively engage in hands-on DIY projects, designing and developing electro-mechanical systems from the ground up. Currently, I am expanding my skill set by learning embedded programming for motor control using microcontrollers. I am always eager to connect and collaborate on topics related to mechanical design, additive manufacturing, and product development.
