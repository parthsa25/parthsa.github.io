I worked with a team for modeling temperature distribution of a rectangular plate. The aim of the model was to develop a MATLAB code to determine temperature field evolution in the plate with help of explicit method. This model was coded in matlab software.

Quantities such as plate dimensions, material properties, ambient temperaturea and details about the heat source. The temperature evolution was calculated using FTCS or forward differnece in time and centered difference in space numerical method.
