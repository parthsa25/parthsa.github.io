Welcome
To my personal projects page

I am passionate about building machines, wether it is a 108L marine engine or state of a art IOT device.
I spent my free time aquiring skills in electrical and programming domain which I can apply to my mechanical designs to make cool machines.
Here is some of my work:

Turntable for 3D scanning

Being an innovation engineer I often need to take a component and make a 3D for it. Having 3D scanners available an idea struck me to build a turntable. Here for the design I have utlitized CATIA V5 as CAD resource, raspberry pi pico microcontroller with a stepper motor and a driver.

The device firmware is programmed in python language for microcontrollers.

Lastly, every part is 3D printed on my customized 3D printer which I will eleborate in next section.

Customizing 3D printer

The backbone to all my prototypes, scratch ideas, rapid prototyping is this customized 3D printer.

I have customized this low cost printer to achieve print quality which is as good as some which costs multiple thousands. I work on reducing mechanical vibrations, structural rigidity of the frame, and currently I am building my own firmware for a custom motherboard.

I have added a raspberry pi zero 2 W for adding IOT capability to my printer along with LED which are PWM controlled and support power on/ off via a relay module.

Work in progress

I am working on building a weather station using a ESP32 microcontroller which supports IOT functions. Secondly, I am investing my time to build a RGB PWM contolled led light. For the same I am building firmware, heatsinks, and mechanical enclosure.
